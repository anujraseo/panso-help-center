Building & scheduling a campaign

Walk the 6-step campaign wizard from audience to send.

Marketing Suite

The campaign builder is a 6-step wizard: Type → Recipients → Set Up → Template → Schedule → Confirm.

  1. Open Marketing Suite → Marketing Campaigns and click Create. Choose Regular (HTML) or Plain text.
  2. Recipients: target by list, tags and segment (combined with AND).
  3. Set Up: name it and set the From sender, subject, preview text and optional UTM tracking.
  4. Template: pick a saved template (or edit one in place).
  5. Schedule: choose a timezone, then Send immediately or Send at a specified date and time. Finish at Confirm to send.
The final Confirm step actually sends the campaign to real recipients — double-check the audience and use Send a test email first.

Before you hit send

  1. Confirm the recipient list/segment and its size.
  2. Send a test email to yourself and check the subject, preview text, personalization and links.
  3. Set the timezone and choose send now or a scheduled date/time.
  4. Only then complete Confirm.

Frequently asked questions

Can I schedule for later?
Yes — at the Schedule step choose Send at a specified date and time and pick a timezone and delivery time.
How do I test it first?
Use Send a test email on the Schedule step to preview the real email in your own inbox.
Can I edit a campaign after it’s sent?
No — a sent campaign is locked. Duplicate it to reuse the setup for a new send.
Where do I see results?
In Marketing Insights and on each campaign card (opens, clicks, unsubscribes).

Reading campaign insights

Track opens, clicks, bounces and unsubscribes for your sends.

Marketing Suite

Marketing Insights shows how your campaigns performed, both in aggregate and per campaign.

  1. Open Marketing Suite → Marketing Insights and set a date range.
  2. Read the summary (Unique Opens %, Unique Clicks %), then pick a campaign to see its full funnel: Sent, Delivered, Opens, Clicks, Bounces, Unsubscribes.

What the numbers mean

  • Delivered vs Sent — a big gap means bounces (bad/unverified addresses).
  • Unique Open % — how compelling your subject line was.
  • Unique Click % — how compelling the content/offer was.
  • Unsubscribes — a spike suggests wrong audience or too-frequent sends.

Frequently asked questions

My open rate is low — what should I change?
Usually the subject line and send time. Try a shorter, clearer subject and test a different slot.
Lots of bounces — why?
Unverified or stale addresses. Target verified subscribers and clean your list.
Clicks are low but opens are high — what does that mean?
People opened but didn’t act — strengthen the offer, the call-to-action button, and the link placement.
